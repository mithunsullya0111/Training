Extensive
Experience

With a team of vastly experienced gastro specialists, Narayana Health is at the forefront of gastro care in India. Our team of doctors is composed of expert gastroenterologists and surgeons from prestigious medical institutions around the world and is supported by a team of highly experienced medical staff.

International
Patient Services

We understand how difficult it can be for critically ill patients and families to travel abroad. At Narayana Health, a dedicated patient coordinator will assist the patient and family every step of the way; from getting estimates to flight booking to arranging food and accommodation.

Key Services & Facilities offered to international patients include:

  • Assistance in getting treatment estimates from qualified doctors
  • Assistance in obtaining medical Visas for patients and attendants
  • Assistance with FRRO and other legal formalities
  • Post-procedure recovery and teleassistance
  • Assistance with flight booking and travel arrangements
  • Translators for patients from Non-English-speaking nations
  • Local accommodation close to the hospital and international cuisine

Narayana Health’s Key Centres for Gastroenterology

Mazumdar Shaw Medical Center, Bangalore

MSMC is an NABH accredited hospital and is among the largest in the region. The hospital is equipped with world-class medical infrastructure and specialises in treating major neurological diseases.

Rabindranath Tagore International Institute Of Cardiac Sciences, Kolkata

RTIICS is among the largest hospitals in the region and is one of the few centres in Eastern India treating advanced Parkinson’s disease and performing Epilepsy surgery. The NABH accredited facility specialises in Minimally Invasive Neuro Surgery and treats all major neurological disorders.

Narayana Superspeciality Hospital, Howrah

Narayana Superspeciality Hospital, Howrah provides comprehensive carein a wide range of medical specialities. With an experienced medical team and world-class equipment, it offers expert neurological care for adults and children.

Delhi, Dharamshila Narayana Superspeciality Hospital

Dharamshila Narayana Superspeciality Hospital is a state-of-the-art superspeciality hospital with world-class infrastructure and an expert team of highly skilled doctors providing comprehensive cancer care. With more than two decades of experience and a long legacy of trust in patient care and innovative treatment approaches, DNSH is emerging as the preferred destination for medical treatment in India.
